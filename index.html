Our Story

From Colombia to Your Cup

San Julian's Coffee Bar brings the authentic taste of Colombian coffee to Winter Park, FL. Our journey began in the lush mountains of Colombia, where we developed a deep appreciation for the art of coffee cultivation and preparation.

At San Julian's, we're committed to providing not just a cup of coffee, but an experience that transports you to the warm, inviting atmosphere of a Colombian coffee farm. Every bean is carefully selected, roasted to perfection, and brewed with love to deliver that distinct Colombian flavor profile.

Direct From Colombian Farms

We source our beans directly from family-owned farms in Colombia's coffee regions, ensuring the highest quality and supporting sustainable farming practices.
